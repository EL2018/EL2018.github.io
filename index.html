MY EXPERIENCE

Oakwood Golf

View Photo Gallery

Why?

I play golf competitively outside of school. I have always been interested in playing for school. This was a great oppurtunity for me to introduce this sport to my school. I went to the head of athletics, Craig Schoof, and asked him where to start. He said I could make a team, but I would have to find 6 other people who would want to join. Or I can play as a Freelance (1-4 players can play). He directed me to the point where I was playing in a tournament with other girls who had the same interests.

The Tournament

How the day went:

Arival

Once I got there, I met my chaperone. We got practice balls and started to practice.

Practice Time

I got there to leave me an hour and a half to practice. I hit on the driving range and then practiced putting.

The Start

They pair you with a couple other girls and call you all up. Each of you take your turn to tee off, just like any other tournament.

To Wrap It Up

You play a round of 18 holes and at the end you all go to the scoring table, sign the score cards, and shake hands.

Full Support

I had Kathleen Swartz come with me as a supervisor. My grandpa and my mom also joined us. You are allowed to have spectators come along with you on the course.

Strategies

  • Get there with enough time to practice
  • Think of it as a good experience and don't take it extremely seriously
  • Enjoy
